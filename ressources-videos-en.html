Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to the most common questions about ADSecure

What video format are ADSecure videos in?

All ADSecure videos are in MP4 format, hosted on YouTube and available in resolution up to 4K. Subtitles in French, Dutch and English are available on each video. You can stream them or download them via YouTube for offline viewing.

How long does it take to learn ADSecure?

You can understand ADSecure basics in 30 minutes (demo + installation). To master all advanced features, allow 4-6 hours of viewing. We also offer live training if you prefer.

How do I access remediation tutorials?

All remediation tutorials are available in the "Tutorials & Guides" section above. Each video explains how to fix a category of vulnerability detected by ADSecure, with concrete examples.

Can I share videos internally?

Absolutely. YouTube videos can be shared with your IT teams using the direct link. No viewing restrictions. You can also embed them in an internal learning system (LMS).

Are subtitles available?

All ADSecure videos include subtitles in French, Dutch and English. You can enable them by clicking the "CC" icon in the YouTube player.

How many videos are available?

We offer more than 50 videos covering all aspects of ADSecure, from installation to advanced use cases. We regularly add new content (2-3 videos per week).
