FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is ADSecure?+
ADSecure is an on-premise Active Directory security auditing software. It analyses your AD infrastructure across 110 security controls and generates two reports: one for management (NIS2/ISO 27001 compliance) and one for IT administrators (step-by-step remediation guides). Everything runs locally — no data leaves your network.
Does ADSecure send data to the cloud?+
No. ADSecure runs entirely on your local machine or a domain-joined Windows workstation. Zero data is transmitted externally. Your directory data never leaves your network. This makes it fully GDPR-compliant by design.
What are the technical requirements?+
Windows 10/11 or Windows Server 2016+, PowerShell 5.1 or later, RSAT Active Directory module installed, a domain member account (read-only AD access is sufficient), and local administrator rights (recommended for registry controls). No internet connection required.
Is ADSecure NIS2 and ISO 27001 compliant?+
Each of ADSecure's 110 controls is mapped to the relevant NIS2 article (EU Directive 2022/2555, mandatory since October 2024) and to ISO 27001:2022 Annexes A.8, A.9, A.12, A.18. The management report automatically generates an Art. 21 compliance summary ready for auditors.
Is it a one-time purchase or a subscription?+
ADSecure is a one-time purchase. No monthly fees, no subscription, no cloud costs. You buy a licence once and use it on your infrastructure. Updates are included for 12 months from the purchase date.

How it works

Deploy. Scan. Report.
In minutes.

01

Deploy

Copy the ADSecure folder to any domain-joined Windows machine. Launch ADSecure-START.bat. RSAT module required.

02

Scan

Click "Start scan". ADSecure queries AD and local system. Estimated duration: 5–15 minutes depending on domain size.

03

Analyse

110 controls execute in sequence. A live progress bar and real-time logs let you track the scan.

04

Report

Two buttons appear: Management Report and IT Report. One click opens them in your browser. Print to PDF instantly.
